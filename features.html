Features

Discover the powerful capabilities of MediaPipe to Blender

Face Tracking

Capture detailed facial expressions with MediaPipe's 468 facial landmarks. The add-on maps these landmarks to your character's facial bones, allowing for nuanced expressions and realistic lip sync.

  • 468 facial landmarks for precise tracking
  • Automatic mapping to facial rigs
  • Capture subtle expressions and micro-movements
  • Real-time lip sync capabilities
  • Eye and eyebrow tracking for complete facial animation

Hand Tracking

Animate hand movements with precision using MediaPipe's 21 hand landmarks per hand. Create natural gestures, sign language, and detailed finger animations without complex rigging or keyframing.

  • 21 landmarks per hand for complete finger tracking
  • Support for both left and right hands simultaneously
  • Capture complex gestures and finger positions
  • Automatic mapping to hand bones
  • Adjustable influence for fine-tuning animations

Body Tracking

Capture full-body movements with MediaPipe's 33 body landmarks. Animate walking, running, dancing, and other physical actions with natural motion and proper weight distribution.

  • 33 body landmarks for complete skeletal tracking
  • Capture natural body movements and postures
  • Works with standard webcams - no special equipment needed
  • Automatic mapping to body armatures
  • Adjustable scale factor for different character proportions

Customizable Mapping

Tailor the motion capture to your specific character rig with flexible mapping options. Choose from automatic detection, preset mappings for popular rigs, or create your own custom mappings.

  • Automatic mapping based on bone names
  • Preset mappings for Rigify and Mixamo rigs
  • Custom mapping editor for complete control
  • Save and load mapping presets
  • Visual feedback for mapping verification

Additional Features

Real-Time Animation

See your animations instantly as you perform them, with minimal latency between your movements and the character's response.

Keyframe Recording

Automatically insert keyframes as you animate, creating editable animation data that can be refined later.

Animation Controls

Fine-tune your animations with smoothing, influence factors, and scale adjustments for perfect results.

Landmark Visualization

Visualize MediaPipe landmarks directly in the Blender viewport for better understanding and debugging.

Easy Integration

Works seamlessly with Blender's animation system, allowing for mixing with other animation techniques.

Open Architecture

Extensible design allows developers to add new features or customize the add-on for specific needs.

System Requirements

Software Requirements

  • Blender: Version 2.93 or newer (Blender 4.0+ recommended)
  • Python: Version 3.7 or newer (included with Blender)
  • Operating System: Windows 10/11, macOS 10.15+, or Linux

Hardware Requirements

  • Webcam: Any standard webcam or video input device
  • Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent (i7 recommended)
  • RAM: 4GB minimum (8GB+ recommended)
  • Graphics: Dedicated GPU recommended for better performance

Note: Performance may vary depending on your hardware configuration, webcam quality, and lighting conditions.
