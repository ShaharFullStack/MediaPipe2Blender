How It Works

1

Capture

MediaPipe detects face, hand, and body landmarks from your webcam in real-time.

2

Map

Landmarks are automatically mapped to your Blender armature bones.

3

Animate

Your movements drive the animation in real-time, with options to record and refine.

The Technology Behind It

MediaPipe to Blender combines Google's MediaPipe machine learning framework with Blender's powerful animation system. The add-on streams landmark data via ZeroMQ for low-latency performance, while providing intuitive controls directly within Blender's interface.
